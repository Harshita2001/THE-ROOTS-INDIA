We, at The Roots India, believe that there comes a point in every individual’s life wherein he/she feels difficult to manage his/her personal goals with the goals of the organization. Since corporate are dealing with humans, it is very important for them to explore and enhance the Emotional Quotient of their employees other than the Intelligence Quotient. We are the only organization using performing arts as a medium for enhancement in a corporate set up. We work towards decreasing the attenuation rate by creating a sense of belongingness for the employees. We work towards creating an environment which not only elevates them as an employee but also at the individual level. Our modules based on performing arts work on communication skills, conflict management, team building, self confidence amongst many other with all segments of an organization, be it HR, Marketing, Finance, or Operations. Our modules are designed for every level of management from top to manager level and also cater to different age groups. With this the employees start experimenting with their ideas, exploring new dimensions, experiencing and expressing their view points which in turn empowers them as well as the organization. If you are looking for such a module for your organization, connect with us at therootssociety@gmail.com.



Communication Building

COMMUNICATION IS THE KEY FEATURE OF ALL THE ORGANIZATIONS. A HEALTHY COMMUNICATION CREATES A POSITIVE ENVIRONMENT. WE FOCUS ON CREATING COMMUNICATION THAT HELPS IN BUILDING TRUST AMONG THE EMPLOYEES. WHEN A GROUP OF PEOPLE WORKING TOGETHER TRUST EACH OTHER, THEY RESPECT EACH OTHER’S SPACE AND TRY TO BE THERE FOR EACH OTHER, WHICH ULTIMATELY RESULTS IN IMPROVED PERFORMANCE OF THE ORGANIZATION AS WHOLE.
