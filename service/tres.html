Idea of education has now been limited to the mark-sheets and it leaves no room for experiment and exploration. We, at The Roots India, believe that the children are the future of the nation and they must learn beyond the four wall structure. Our modules aim at experiential learning and using alternative education means to give the children the opportunity to experiment with their ideas, explore their surroundings, experience new situations and express their viewpoints. We work towards the overall development of a child and instill an evolving thought process to make them liberal citizens of this developing Nation. Our modules are designed for a varied range of age groups and deal with the issues belonging to that particular group only. Issues like Bullying, Sex Education, and Women are some of the areas where we work using alternate ways of learning. - Students Training Our artistic techniques are designed on an interesting module of theatrics inculcated with performing arts approach that helps children to attain academic knowledge, along with social and emotional development. The all-round development enriches and motivates the learning experience fundamentally. The aim of The Roots India is to promote arts in education. For this, our methodology focuses on the growth and development of a child, letting him/her explore the values of different aspects of life, making him capable to opt for his personal choices, by discovering his own abilities and interests. - Teacher Training: According to our belief, things are rooted inside each and every being. And for the complete nourishment of these roots, teachers being the convener, play a crucial role while watering the tiny seeds. Therefore, understanding the importance of teachers as an important milestone of growth in a child’s learning experience;The Roots India presents a special teacher training program. The nourishment of our children’s guidance spirits is really very crucial for the future of our young minds. Our training for the teachers, inculcates artistic techniques for the creative upbringing of the bundle of joy under their guidance. - Parent Training Communication is an important bridge between parent-teacher relationships, for the growth of a young mind. The Roots India’s initiative also tries to come up with a platform of opportunities for the parents to communicate, discuss and come together at a forum with their personal viewpoint, suggestions and problems regarding their dealing with the young ones. For an enhancement module catering to the needs of your school, contact us at therootssociety@gmail.com.
