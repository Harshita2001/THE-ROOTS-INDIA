We, at The Roots India, believe that humans do not need to be ‘trained’. They need a medium to explore themselves and thus we promote the idea of exploration in the routinised life that is led by most of us. With the tailor made enhancement programmes that we offer individual experiments, explores, experiences, expresses and empower not only self but also the people around. Methodology- The Roots India is Training and an Enhancement Studio for more than 10years now. Our modules are creatively made after discourses and discussions with experienced theatre artists and HRs to make sure that no aspect is left behind. Our modules are tailor made and are flexible at the same time for different age groups and different organizations. Performing Arts is used as the methodology to attain the experience, experiment and explore, express and empower. If you are looking for an enhancement module for yourself or your organization, contact us at therootssociety@gmail.com.
