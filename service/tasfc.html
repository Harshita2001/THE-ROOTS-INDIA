This programme is a sequel to the Part-time course and the Weekend Workshop providing students the opportunity to further sharpen their skills by training in the advanced modules of acting offered in this course, while allowing them to maintain their regular personal schedules. It draws together an eclectic group of people ranging from young working professionals to people who are well-established in busy careers. No. of Sessions: 24 (approximately) Total Course Duration: 3 months, 12 weekends Schedule: Saturdays and Sundays only Age Criteria: 16 years and above Direct Admission. No audition and interviews to be conducted. For registering, connect with us at therootssociety@gmail.com.
